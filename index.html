Why the Change?


The Persistent Problem

In today's retail world, outdated checkout systems bog down performance. This results in unhappy customers and, consequently, declining retention rates. The typical pain points? Long queues, malfunctioning self-checkouts, and issues with items like fresh produce that don't have barcodes.

The Peak Hour Chaos

Peak shopping hours only amplify these problems, inflating labor costs for retailers. Add to this the health concerns in a post-COVID-19 world due to shared checkout keypads.

The Need for Revolution

All these issues culminate in a shopping experience that's far from ideal. It tarnishes a retailer's reputation and erodes customer trust. The industry needs a comprehensive solution - one that addresses these pain points head-on. This is where CogniCart steps in, poised to redefine the retail checkout experience.
